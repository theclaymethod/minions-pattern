# Explore without bloating the planner

Use Luna xhigh, Sonnet, or Haiku for bounded repository reading, search, dependency tracing, and context gathering. Escalate GPT exploration to Sol High when Luna's synthesis is inadequate or the decision is high risk.

1. Ask a narrow question. Name the repository, entry points, exclusions, and required evidence.
2. Keep the scout read-only and prohibit implementation.
3. Require a compact map: relevant paths and symbols, data/control flow, searches performed, uncertainties, and recommended next reads.
4. Let the host verify decisive anchors before planning.
5. Skip adversarial review for raw search results unless they drive a high-risk architecture or security decision.

Stop when the question is answered. Do not combine exploration and implementation in one dispatch.
